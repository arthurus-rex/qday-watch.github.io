[October 21st, 2025] The JVG algorithm

Red flags are that it was published on Preprint.org instead of arxiv.org, the article is not peer reviewed (although that is increasingly common these days), the authors had a conflict of interest that they did not disclose, and the core tenant of their argument relied on a misunderstanding of computational complexity analysis of Shor's algorithm. Scott Aaronson's blog does a good job of describing the problems with JVG concisely. If you want entertainment, you can read this Reddit thread.
